FAQs

What is BaseBulls?

A premium NFT project on Base blockchain, featuring 10,000 pixel art bulls with unique personalities, traits, and utility. Each bull is procedurally generated with over 150 possible traits across multiple categories.

Where can I buy BaseBulls?

You can mint directly on our website or trade on major marketplaces like OpenSea and MagicEden. The $BULL token is available on Uniswap (Base chain).

What's the utility?

BaseBulls provide exclusive access to community events, governance rights, staking opportunities with the $BULL token, merchandise drops, and upcoming metaverse integrations. Rarer bulls come with enhanced benefits.

What is Base?

Base is an Ethereum Layer 2 blockchain developed by Coinbase, designed for fast and affordable decentralized applications and NFTs. It provides the security of Ethereum with significantly lower transaction costs and faster confirmation times.

How can I get involved?

Join our Discord and Telegram communities, follow us on Twitter, participate in our weekly AMAs, and attend our virtual and IRL events. Holding a BaseBull NFT grants you full access to all community activities and governance.
